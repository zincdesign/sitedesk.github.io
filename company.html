Sitedesk

Construction BIM software and services to help companies connect BIM data

Sitedesk is an independent UK software and services company based in Manchester. It was founded in 2013 and provides a fast, flexible and easy-to-use collaborative working environment for those involved in construction and engineering projects. Sitedesk software is a SaaS solution that helps users manage BIM (Building Information Modelling) and traditional construction projects.

Today Sitedesk is run by a professional management team with deep knowledge of software and services, extensive experience in developing collaborative solutions, and a track record of supporting the construction and engineering sectors in the UK and internationally.
