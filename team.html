  • 2009-2011

    Michael McCullen, Chairman

    Michael joined Sitedesk in July 2015. He was formerly Chairman of Asta Development plc, a leading international project management software business, of which he was a co-founder. As managing director he was responsible for the growth strategy and ultimate sale of that business in 2006, and sat on the board of its new parent company, Eleco plc, until founding his own consultancy practice in 2015. Michael holds a Computer Science degree and an MBA.

  • March 2011

    Rob Umphray, Director of BIM

    Rob has been with Sitedesk since 2013. As director of BIM he supports Sitedesk customers to identify and define the priority goals for their BIM projects; he then helps them implement Sitedesk software to deliver these objectives by improving project visibility, productivity and cost efficiency. He has built a deep and passionate interest in BIM, in particular around the challenges of workflow improvement in the expanded BIM value chain. Rob joined Sitedesk from TestPlant, an award-winning international software business, where he helped major technology companies around the world achieve cost savings and enhance operational delivery via automation technologies and process improvement methodologies. He brings a blended sales and services background which includes many years delivering organisational change projects for executive search specialist Oryx.
